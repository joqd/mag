# Why I Chose NuxtJS for This Blog and Resume Website

I chose **NuxtJS** for this project because it feels like the perfect fit for what I wanted to build: a modern, clean, and flexible website for my blog and resume. I love how Nuxt combines simplicity with power. It gives me a smooth developer experience while still allowing me to create something beautiful and professional.

One of the main reasons I enjoy NuxtJS is that it makes building pages feel natural and organized. I like the structure it provides, the way it supports Vue easily, and how it helps me create both static and dynamic content without unnecessary complexity. For a personal website, that balance is exactly what I need.

For this project, I’m using a modern stack that includes NuxtJS, Vue, TypeScript, and Tailwind-like styling through the UI framework I’ve integrated into the site. I also use Markdown content for posts, which makes writing and managing articles feel simple and enjoyable. That combination lets me focus on the content and the experience rather than getting lost in configuration.

What I really love about NuxtJS is that it feels practical and expressive at the same time. It helps me build a site that is fast, structured, and easy to maintain, while still giving me the freedom to shape it in my own way. That is why I chose it for this blog and resume website, and why I keep enjoying working with it.
